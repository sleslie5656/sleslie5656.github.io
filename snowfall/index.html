Donner Summit Snowfall Data 1879 - 2016

Central Sierra Snowfall

This is an interactive visualization of the total snowfall and maximum snow depth for the last 138 years. Hover over bars to view the values for each year and click the buttons to adjust sorting options.

The data was compiled by Central Sierra Snow Laboratory, with data from 1879-1945 and 1953-1957 collected by Southern Pacific Railroad, and data from 1946-1952 and 1958-2013 collected by Central Sierra Snow Laboratory.

The graph and interactions are created using D3.
