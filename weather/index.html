Tahoe Weather Dashboard

This page is an experimental dashboard providing access to the resources I typically look at regarding weather. Sites that are iframe-friendly are shown as purple buttons and will open within the page for easy comparison with other sites. Sites that prevent themselves from being opened in iframes are represented as blue buttons and will open in a separate tab.

I live in Reno and typically hit up Mt Rose Ski Area, so the sites are configured to be specific to that general area.
